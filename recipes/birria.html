Birria Tacos

How to Make Birria Tacos

Birria Tacos are a tradional Mexican dish including lamb meat, cheese and spices

Ingredients

  • Lamb Beef
  • Seasoinings
  • Cheese
  • Chillis
  • Tomatoes
  • Tortillas
    •

    Steps

    1. Season the beef and sear on both sides
    2. Boil and soak the chillis for 20 minutes
    3. Blend the soaked chillis, tomatoes and seasonings
    4. Strain the sauce and pour over the meat
    5. Cover the Dutch oven and cook until the meat begins to fall apart, basting often
    6. Allow meat to cook on high for four hours
    7. Allow the meat to rest, then shred
    8. Warm the tortillas on the griddle
    9. Combine the meat and cheese on tortillas and serve with desired toppings
