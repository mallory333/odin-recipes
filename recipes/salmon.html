Oven Baked Salmon

How to Make Salmon

There are many different ways to make salmon, but the healthiest and most effecient way is to bake the salmon with different herbs and spices

Ingredients

  • 12 oz salmon filet
  • Seasoinings
  • Lemon
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
    •

    Steps

    1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees
    2. Season filet with salt and pepper
    3. Finely chop garlic and place all over filet
    4. Add a half squeezed lemon juice on filet
    5. Cook for 15 minutes or until heated throughly
    6. Add more lemon juice if needed
    7. Top with thyme and serve
